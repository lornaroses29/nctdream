WHAT IS NCT?

NCT (Neo Culture Technology) is a South Korean boy band formed by SM Entertainment, introduced in January 2016. The group operates under an "open" concept — no fixed number of members — currently consisting of 26 members across six sub-units.

01
NCT U

Rotational sub-unit. Members are selected per each release to match the concept and song.

02
NCT 127

Seoul-based unit named after the longitude of Seoul. Known for powerful performances and experimental sound.

03
NCT DREAM

Youth-oriented unit that became permanent in 2020. Famous for energetic, trendy concepts.

04
WAY V

Chinese sub-unit managed by SM's Label V. Promotes primarily in Chinese-speaking markets.
